1. The Strategic Executive Summary
Focus: Business growth, market positioning, and ROI. This project evaluates Porter Delivery’s operational data to transform logistics challenges into competitive advantages. By analyzing the intersection of delivery speed, order value, and partner availability, the study identifies high-impact opportunities to increase revenue and customer loyalty. The findings provide a roadmap for strategic staffing and store partnerships, ensuring Porter maximizes profitability in a high-growth market by aligning resource allocation with real-world demand patterns.

2. The Operations & Logistics Brief
Focus: Efficiency, bottlenecks, and resource management. This study utilizes advanced Excel modeling—including regression and correlation analysis—to dissect the delivery lifecycle. It examines how order complexity, store category, and peak-hour staffing levels dictate overall performance. By pinpointing specific causes of delivery delays, the project offers actionable solutions for optimizing partner schedules and improving store-level efficiency. The goal is a streamlined supply chain that minimizes friction and enhances delivery throughput.

3. The Modern Tech/Data Analytics Perspective
Focus: Data-driven decision making and "Big Data" insights. Leveraging a robust dataset of delivery metrics, this project applies statistical functions and PivotTable analysis to uncover hidden correlations in Porter’s marketplace. From identifying high-value customer segments to mapping market-specific behaviors, the analysis moves beyond descriptive reporting into diagnostic insights. This data-backed approach empowers stakeholders to implement dynamic pricing, targeted marketing, and predictive resource management to stay ahead in the food delivery tech space.

Key Highlights (Consolidated)
The Problem: Inefficient delivery times and untapped revenue potential.

The Tools: Excel (Regression, PivotTables, Statistical Correlation).

The Solution: Data-driven staffing, optimized store partnerships, and tiered pricing strategies.

The Goal: Improved customer satisfaction and a stronger competitive edge.
